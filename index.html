About The Analyst

From commerce ledgers to cosmic dashboards — the story of a data navigator.

My journey began at Al-Azhar University, where I pursued a Commerce degree in the English Section — graduating ranked 4th in my Accounting Department. But behind every balance sheet, I saw something more powerful: patterns, trends, and stories hiding in numbers.

That realization launched me into the world of Data Analytics and Business Intelligence. I mastered SQL, Python, and Power BI — not just as tools, but as instruments to decode the universe of business data and translate it into decisions that move organizations forward.

Today I design ETL pipelines, Star Schema data models, and interactive Power BI dashboards that reduce errors, boost revenue, and give businesses the clarity they need to navigate uncertainty — just like a pilot charting a course through deep space.

🎯 Career Vision: Growing into a Senior Data / BI role where I can architect end-to-end analytics ecosystems — pairing financial acumen with engineering rigor to help organizations make faster, evidence-based decisions.
